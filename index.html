About EduFarm

Empowering Communities Through Education & Sustainable Development

EduFarm is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering communities through education, sustainable agriculture, youth development, and environmental stewardship.

We believe that every individual deserves access to quality education, practical skills, and opportunities that create lasting social and economic impact.
